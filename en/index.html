About

A legal partner you can trust

Bianca Rohozneanu is an attorney in Cluj-Napoca, a member of the Cluj Bar Association, providing legal advice, assistance and representation to both individuals and businesses.

Her work focuses on identifying clear, effective legal solutions tailored to the specifics of each situation. She offers legal advice, assistance and representation before the courts, public authorities and other competent institutions, in areas such as civil law, family law, employment law, administrative litigation, urban planning and land development, corporate law, and proceedings before the National Trade Register Office.

Her professional approach is built on open communication, careful analysis of each legal situation and a realistic assessment of the available options. Every client receives a strategy tailored to their goals and legal support throughout the entire procedure.

She is a graduate of the Faculty of Law at Babeș-Bolyai University in Cluj-Napoca and of the Master's programme in International Business Law at the same institution. Her academic training was complemented by a study experience at Radboud University in Nijmegen, the Netherlands, fostering an international perspective on the law and a deeper understanding of European legal systems.

Practice areas

Areas of expertise

Comprehensive legal services — from advice and drafting of documents to representation before courts and authorities.

Civil law

Assistance, advice and representation in civil disputes and relations: performance of contractual obligations, civil liability, debt recovery, enforcement, claims and other civil matters — including cases with foreign elements and the application of private international law.

Family law

Advice, assistance and representation in divorce, establishing and modifying child support, exercise of parental authority, visitation schedules with minors, division of joint assets, protection orders and other family disputes.

Inheritance

Advice, assistance and representation in inheritance matters and probate proceedings: establishing heir status, settling the estate, division of the estate, disputes between heirs and other aspects concerning inheritance rights.

Real estate law

Advice and representation in property rights and legal matters concerning real estate: title checks, sale-purchase and lease agreements, sale promises, co-ownership, easements, boundary setting, claims, eviction, division of property and ownership disputes.

Employment law

Legal support for employers and employees: drafting and reviewing individual employment contracts, termination of employment, disciplinary proceedings, dismissals, recovery of salary rights and representation in labour disputes.

Corporate law & Trade Register

Incorporation of companies, amendment of constitutive documents, transfer of shares, change of directors or registered office, share capital increases, dissolution and deregistration of companies, and representation in proceedings before the National Trade Register Office.

Administrative litigation & planning

Relations with public authorities and administrative litigation: annulment of administrative acts, challenging fines, unjustified refusals to resolve requests, permits and approvals. Includes urban planning and land development — planning certificates, building/demolition permits, planning documentation (PUG, PUZ, PUD) and related disputes.

Contracts & legal counsel

Drafting, review and negotiation of civil and commercial contracts, issuing legal opinions, assessing contractual risks and identifying suitable legal solutions to prevent disputes and allow business to be conducted safely.
