About

Bianca Rohozneanu is an attorney based in Cluj-Napoca, a member of the Cluj Bar Association, providing legal advice and court representation to individuals and businesses.

Her approach is built on clarity, honesty and practical solutions: every client receives a realistic assessment of their situation and a strategy tailored to their goals.

Practice areas

Civil law

Advice and representation in civil disputes: contracts, civil liability, property matters and debt recovery.

Commercial law

Legal support for companies: drafting and negotiating commercial contracts, disputes between businesses, and day-to-day business relations.

Family law

Divorce, child custody, child support and division of property — sensitive matters handled with discretion and empathy.
